Rules for Letterle

  • - You must guess a 5 letter word within 6 guesses. -
  • - If you guess a letter that is in the word and in the correct position, the letter will light up green -
  • - If you guess a letter that is in the word but not in the correct position, the letter will light up yellow -
  • - If you guess a letter that is not in the word, the letter will turn grey -
